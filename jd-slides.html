Introducing the Platform Solutions Architect

Bringing AI and automation together to transform how we work — and how we serve our clients.

Why This Role Matters Now

  • Technology is evolving faster than our processes.
  • AI and non-AI automation offer new ways to save time, cut costs, and unlock growth.
  • Our industry is at a point where the organizations who integrate these tools effectively will lead the market.

Core Purpose of the Role

I design and build creative, practical solutions that combine AI and non-AI automation to:

  • Improve internal efficiency.
  • Enhance customer experience.
  • Enable entirely new products and services.

What This Looks Like in Practice

  • Strategy & Discovery – Identifying high-impact opportunities across the business and client projects.
  • Design & Development – Building scalable platforms and workflows.
  • Implementation & Adoption – Rolling out solutions smoothly and training users.
  • Measurement & Optimization – Tracking results and refining for greater impact.

Industry-Relevant Impact Examples

  • Time savings: Automating repetitive admin tasks frees up staff for higher-value work.
  • Cost reduction: Streamlining workflows reduces waste and error rates.
  • Customer satisfaction: Faster response times and more personalized experiences.
  • Scalability: New solutions that let us handle more clients without adding overhead.

How This Role Will Work

  • Collaborative project selection with leadership.
  • Hands-on design, development, and deployment of solutions.
  • Continuous learning to stay ahead of both AI and non-AI automation trends.

This role is not just about technology — it’s about reimagining what’s possible.

Together, we can build systems that make work smoother, clients happier, and growth more sustainable.
