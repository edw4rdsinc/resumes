Platform Solutions Architect

Job Description

Summary

I design and build creative, practical solutions that combine AI and non-AI automation to transform how we work — internally and for our clients. My focus is on spotting opportunities, imagining what’s possible, and turning those ideas into working systems that save time, improve experiences, and open new ways to grow.

Responsibilities

Strategy & Discovery

  • ✓ Partner with leadership to identify high-impact opportunities for AI and automation.
  • ✓ Analyze existing processes, uncover inefficiencies, and reimagine them through solutions-focused problem-solving.
  • ✓ Keep a pulse on emerging technologies, testing new tools and methods that could benefit the business and its clients.

Design & Development

  • ✓ Architect and build scalable platforms, workflows, and integrations that improve operational efficiency.
  • ✓ Create systems that are robust, adaptable, and easy to maintain as needs evolve.
  • ✓ Ensure solutions are user-friendly and accessible to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Implementation & Adoption

  • ✓ Collaborate with teams and clients to deploy solutions, ensuring smooth rollout and minimal disruption.
  • ✓ Train users on new tools and processes, tailoring communication to their needs and comfort level.
  • ✓ Gather feedback to refine and improve solutions post-launch.

Measurement & Optimization

  • ✓ Track and report on the impact of each solution, from time saved to improved customer satisfaction.
  • ✓ Continuously refine systems based on performance data and user feedback.
  • ✓ Identify opportunities to expand successful solutions to other parts of the business or client base.
